§ 01 — Philosophy

Craft over
commodity.
Always.

The builders of medieval cathedrals knew they would never see their work completed. They carved detail into stone arches that no human eye would ever see from below — because the work itself demanded it.

That's the standard we hold. Every database schema, every API route, every line of TypeScript is load-bearing. We don't build what looks good in a demo. We build what works at 2am on a Saturday when everything is live.

18 years of building things that last. One principle that never changes: infrastructure compounds, shortcuts decay.
